EMC Practice Exam

Directions:

  • This test consists of 25 questions. These questions are similar in topic and style to the ones you will see on March 27th.
  • On the real exam, you are only allowed to use a writing utensil, scratch paper, and your big brain. However, on this practice exam, you can use whatever resources you wish to use.
  • The problems are not necessarily in order of difficulty.
  • If you don't know how to do a problem, don't look at the solution right away. Try some different things first.
  • Most importantly, be creative, and have fun. The most important thing is that you exercise your brain thinking about these problems-- even if you get them wrong, the time spent thinking is very valuable.
